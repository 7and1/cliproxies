## FAQ Section (Apps Page)

### Common Questions

**Q: Are these apps official CLIProxyAPI projects?**

A: Most ecosystem apps are community-built and maintained. They integrate with CLIProxyAPI but are developed independently. Check each app's repository for details.

**Q: How do I get my app listed?**

A: Submit a pull request to the CLIProxies repository. See the "Submit Your App" section above for requirements.

**Q: Are these apps free to use?**

A: All apps in the directory are open source. Most are free, but some may have premium features. Check individual app licenses for details.

**Q: Which app should I use?**

A: It depends on your workflow:

- macOS users: Try VibeProxy for menu bar access
- Windows users: ProxyPilot offers native Windows support
- Developers: Claude Proxy VSCode integrates with your editor
- CLI users: CCS for account switching
